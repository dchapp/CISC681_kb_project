# Tachycardia rule
heart rate > 100 ==> tachycardia
# Jaundice rule
yellow skin or eyes ==> jaundice
abdominal pain & nausea & vomiting & disorientation & jaundice ==> liver failure
altered mental state & myoclonus & seizures ==> encephalopathy
immense thirst & frequent urination & pain on/around kidneys ==> kidney failure
